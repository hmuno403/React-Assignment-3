Baku, the cosmopolitan capital of Azerbaijan, is a fascinating city where ancient history and modern innovation converge along the Caspian Sea. This dynamic metropolis features a striking skyline, including the iconic Flame Towers, which light up the night with their mesmerizing displays. Baku's UNESCO-listed Old City, Icheri Sheher, transports visitors to a bygone era, with its winding cobblestone streets, the medieval Maiden Tower, and the well-preserved Palace of the Shirvanshahs. The city is also home to an array of museums, galleries, and theaters, showcasing Azerbaijani art, history, and culture. With its intriguing blend of old-world charm and contemporary sophistication, Baku offers a captivating and memorable travel experience.
