Cairo, the bustling capital of Egypt, is a mesmerizing fusion of ancient history and modern vibrancy. Home to the iconic Great Pyramids of Giza and the enigmatic Sphinx, Cairo transports visitors to a time of pharaohs and legendary civilizations. As you explore the city, you'll discover the magnificent treasures of the Egyptian Museum, the vibrant colors and scents of the Khan El-Khalili bazaar, and the stunning architecture of the Citadel of Saladin and the Al-Azhar Mosque. This captivating city along the Nile River invites you to uncover the mysteries of its rich past while experiencing the energy of contemporary Egyptian culture.
